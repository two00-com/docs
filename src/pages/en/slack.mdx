# Add a Slack Webhook

If you want to receive notifications in your Slack Channel, you can set them up as follows.

- Go to [https://api.slack.com/apps](https://api.slack.com/apps) and click **Create New App**.

<ModalImage
  className="img-small"
  small={SNAImage}
  large={SNAImage}
  alt="Add new app"
  hideDownload
/>

- Now enter a **Name** and a **Workspace**:

<ModalImage
  className="img-small"
  small={SCNAImage}
  large={SCNAImage}
  alt="Create new app"
  hideDownload
/>

- In the section **Add features and functionality** click the **Incoming Webhook** button:

<ModalImage
  className="img-small"
  small={SAddIWImage}
  large={SAddIWImage}
  alt="Add incoming webhook"
  hideDownload
/>

- Click **Activate Incoming Webhooks**:

<ModalImage
  className="img-small"
  small={SAIWImage}
  large={SAIWImage}
  alt="Activate incoming webhook"
  hideDownload
/>

- Click **Add New Webhook to Workspace**:

<ModalImage
  className="img-small"
  small={SAddNIWImage}
  large={SAddNIWImage}
  alt="Add incoming webhook to the workspace"
  hideDownload
/>

- Confirm that the webhook is allowed to post in this channel:

<ModalImage
  className="img-small"
  small={SAWTPImage}
  large={SAWTPImage}
  alt="Confirm that the webhook is allowed to post to the channel"
  hideDownload
/>

- Copy the webhook URL and save it in the project

  className="img-small"
  small={SCWImage}
  large={SCWImage}
  alt="Copy the webhook URL"
  hideDownload
/>

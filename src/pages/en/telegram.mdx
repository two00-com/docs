# Add Telegram Notifications

If you want to receive notifications in a Telegram group, you can set this up as follows.

- Create a new Telegram group or open an existing one (you must be an administrator of the group to add a bot).

<ModalImage
  className="img-small"
  small={TCGImage}
  large={TCGImage}
  alt="Create new group"
  hideDownload
/>

- Click on the name of your chat to get to the settings and then click on **Add Member**:

<ModalImage
  className="img-small"
  small={TAMImage}
  large={TAMImage}
  alt="Add new member"
  hideDownload
/>

- Search for the bot named **two00bot** and add it to the group:

<ModalImage
  className="img-small"
  small={TATImage}
  large={TATImage}
  alt="Add two00bot"
  hideDownload
/>

- After you have added the bot, it will appear in the group's member list:

<ModalImage
  className="img-small"
  small={TABImage}
  large={TABImage}
  alt="Add two00bot to the memberslist"
  hideDownload
/>

- Copy your `project key` on `<Ihr Organisationsname>.two00.com` in your project:

<ModalImage
  className="img-small"
  small={TCPKImage}
  large={TCPKImage}
  alt="Copy project key"
  hideDownload
/>

- Write **/start &lt;UUID&gt;** followed by your project key:

<ModalImage
  className="img-small"
  small={TABImage}
  large={TABImage}
  alt="Start Listening to your project"
  hideDownload
/>

You will now receive your notifications in this telegram group for your project.
